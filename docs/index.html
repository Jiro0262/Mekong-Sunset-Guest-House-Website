An authentic hideaway

Welcome to Mekong Sunset Guest House, perfectly situated just moments from the Mekong, our home is the ideal base for exploring the wonders of Cambodia, while savoring homemade cuisine crafted from fresh local market ingredients.

When the green season arrives, the refuge truly comes alive. Spend your days diving into refreshing waters or casting a line for a peaceful afternoon of fishing. As daylight fades, prepare to be mesmerized by spectacular, golden sunsets—all while being just a 5-minute drive from the heart of town. It’s the perfect blend of wild escape and modern convenience.
